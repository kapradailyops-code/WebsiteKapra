# Kapra Web AI Landing Page

## Executive Summary
A premium, cinematic one-page marketing site for Kapra Web AI Makers Pvt. Ltd. The site serves as the primary digital storefront, showcasing web, mobile, and AI engineering services through high-end webGL/canvas animations, scroll-driven storytelling, and interactive 3D elements.

## Target Audience
- Ambitious brands and enterprises seeking high-quality, scalable digital products and AI integration.
- Stakeholders assessing technical competence based on the presentation layer.

## Core Value Proposition
- "Scalable Web, Mobile, & AI Development for Ambitious Brands"
- Demonstrates engineering capability through an extremely polished, high-performance UI (Framer Motion, GSAP, Three.js).
- Frictionless contact flow integrated directly with HubSpot for CRM ingestion. 

## Key Success Metrics
- **Performance**: High frame rate across devices for heavy canvas/WebGL sequences.
- **Conversion**: Successful submission and HubSpot tracking of inquiry forms.
- **Visual Impact**: Flawless execution of dark/light mode, scroll animations, and text reveals without FOUC or jank.
